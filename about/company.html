about JimBaub Interactive

Founded in November 2021, JimBaub Interactive set out to bring to market the unfinished projects and half baked ideas of a games dev graduate with too much time on their hands. Evolving from the acquisition of a .tech domain that needed something put on it, jimbaub.tech was born and houses the JimBaub Interactive website as part of the wider JimBaub Media Group from Twitch, Facebook, Youtube & Altair streamer JimBaub. The games that JimBaub Interactive seeks to bring to market hope to end the current obsession with freemium games where you pay $60 for a game to have to turn around and drop more money on ingame currency to get the "full" experience. It's the hope of JimBaub Interactive to make the industry question its norms and restore the stus quo to how it was when games were released finished and the content added in new updates was guaranteed. It's become a point of uniqueness that your $60 game has "free content updates", when we believe it should be the norm. So come join us on our mission to make games that people love to play and where buyers remorse is no more.
